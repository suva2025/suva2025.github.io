About Me

I am Dr. Suvajit Saha — academician by profession, photographer by passion. With over 12 years of experience in microbiology, molecular biology, and public health research, I specialize in understanding diarrheal diseases and the environmental drivers behind them.

My doctoral work at ICMR-NICED, Kolkata, focused on Vibrio dynamics and antimicrobial resistance in the Indian Ganges Delta and Gulf of Gujarat. I have mentored postgraduate students, taught undergraduate courses, and contributed to national and international collaborative research.

I’m passionate about translating science from bench to public benefit and documenting the natural world through my lens, capturing stories where science and nature meet.
